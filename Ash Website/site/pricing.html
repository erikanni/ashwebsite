If you are wanting a greyscale with some added colors, it is an extra $1 per color.

Items/accessories:

Simple accessories (less detail or intricate designs) will have a base price of $5 per item/accessory. Accessories can be weapons, staffs, or anything handheld larger than a phone.

Intricate accessories (more detailed, with textures or designs) will be priced at $7.

*Accessories that are a part of the character design are exempt from extra charges (such as Thanos with an Infinity Gauntlet)
